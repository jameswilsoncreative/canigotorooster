Parking & Info

Parking Fees

Day-Use Fee $10.00 / car
Annual Pass $60.00 / year

Where to Park: East side of park, all the way at the end.

Paying the Fee: Payable at the booth. If closed, use the automated card machine (credit cards only).

Clothing-optional Beach Access: Park on the far east of the parking lot and walk down the hill. If you want to go to sand island, take the beach trail. If you want the mainland "gay" beach section, take the lagoon trail.
